About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower cardamom growers with the tools they need to safeguard their crops and achieve optimal yields. By providing an accurate and accessible disease detection platform, we aim to revolutionize the cultivation of cardamom, ensuring healthy plants, sustainable practices, and a thriving agricultural community.

Our Technology

Our disease detection model is powered by cutting-edge deep learning techniques, including DenseNet, InceptionV3, and EfficientNetV2B0 architectures. These advanced models have been meticulously trained on a vast dataset of cardamom leaf images, enabling them to accurately identify and classify various diseases with exceptional precision.
